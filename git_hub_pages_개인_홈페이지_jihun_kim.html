About

I am a mathematician working in differential geometry, with a focus on Riemannian geometry and submanifold theory. I am particularly interested in hypersurfaces, curvature conditions, and geometric structures related to Einstein manifolds.

Research Interests

  • Riemannian and differential geometry
  • Submanifold theory and locally symmetric hypersurfaces
  • Einstein geometry, weakly Einstein and 2-Stein manifolds
  • Curvature conditions and rigidity phenomena

Current topics include the study of locally symmetric hypersurfaces in globally symmetric spaces and geometric characterisations of special curvature conditions.
